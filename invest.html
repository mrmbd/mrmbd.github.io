We start, buy and invest in online first brands that we love.

Why Bombay?

Experience

We’ve been working with online brands for a couple of years now. And to be honest we like it more and more. Launch, measure, adjust and repeat. Gotta stay agile in this type of business!

We have already been through a couple of deals before and thus know what to look out for.

Culture

For us, working together with people one likes is key. There must be that initial good old gut feeling that feels just right. This drives us forward and is one of the single most important things to us.

We will not seek to overthrow your current operations nor are we looking to put in new managers.

Fast

We are a small team and we want to keep it that way. We want to keep moving fat. Expect fast, concrete and honest answers from us.

Because we want to move fast we are not looking for complicated deal structures. Simple straightforward offers are what we aim for.

Typical company specs

- 2 - 5 years existence
- Profitable
- Simple business
- Strong team

Become a scout

Work for a Bombay company

You don’t own a business but are interested in setting up and running businesses? Contact us to see if there is that magic fit we are all looking for.
