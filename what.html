Websites & stores

Online brands thrive because of unique products and a clear vision. But also because they are well built, robust and beautiful digital interfaces.


We do everything from simple websites to international multi language and multi currency e-commerce.

1. Design

We listen to your story with the goal to deeply understand your brand and needs. We then create wireframes and later build a beautiful design around that.

2. Develop

In this stage we find the best possible technical implementation to make sure your site is not only a joy to look at but also responsive, fast, safe and interactive. We mostly use Lightspeed and Shopify.

3. Launch & scale

We help you launch the thing but also track how it performs and what we could do in the future to make it even better. Agility is key in the fast pace world that we currently live in.

Lightspeed

Lightspeed aims at the slightly bigger clients that might have some physical stores next to their online presence.

Shopify

Shopify is a very good e-commerce platform with a very rich ecosystem of apps so that will help you create the online presence you need.

Online marketing & branding

Finding a sound position in the market is extremely important. We can help you analyse where you are and help you decide where you want to go next. We can also help you create visual assets (images and video) because as they are the cornerstone of a good online marketing strategy.

1. Brand analysis

To make sure we understand the current situation we’ll have a look at all the data that you have available. We’ll help you structure it so we can draw conclusions. Metrics we look at: SEO, SEA and Socials.

2. Brand potential

Now that we know where we are we can help. Decide where to go next and make it concrete. This is a step we need to do together of course so it is thoroughly aligned with the goals of the business.

3. Photography

People love great images. We can help you create the images that you need to make your brand stand out.

4. Implementation

We can implement everything for you but we can also help you implement it yourself.
