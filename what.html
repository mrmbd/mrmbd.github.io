Websites & Stores

1. Design

We listen to you story with the goal to deeply understand what you need. We make that concrete by putting it into a wireframe and later build a beautiful design around that.

2. Develop

In this stage we have to make sure we find the best possible technical implementation to make sure your site is not only a joy to look at but also responsive, fast and interactive. We mostly use Lightspeed and Shopify.

3. Launch & scale

We will help you launch the thing but also see how it performs qnd what we could do in the future to make it even better. Agility is key in the fast pace that we currently live in.

Online brands thrive because of unique products and a clear vision.

But also because of they are well built, robust, fast and beautiful digital interfaces.

We have a lot of expertise in international ecomm. Multi currency/language setups are our thing.

Online marketing & branding

1. Current brand

To make sure we understand the current situation we’ll have a look at all the data that you have available. We’ll help you Structure it so we can draw conclusions. Things we look at: SEO, SEA and Socials.

2. Potential brand

Now that we know where we are we can help. Decide where to go next and make it concrete. This is a step we need to do together of course!

3. Implement

We can help you implement everything but we can also help you implement it yourself.
